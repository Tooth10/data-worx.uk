Delivery example

A typical “messy-to-meaningful” engagement looks like this:

  • Audit & triage: what’s trusted, what’s broken, what’s missing
  • Define KPIs & rules: consistent measures, clear definitions, no “two versions of the truth”
  • Build the pipeline: cleaning, QA checks, automated refresh
  • Deliver the output: dashboard + a short “so what?” briefing that leaders actually use

Outcome: fewer manual steps, faster reporting cycles, and decisions made on trusted numbers.
